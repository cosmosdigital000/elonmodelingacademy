Our Story

From the Heart of
Sasaram to the
Sacred Ghats

Our journey began modestly in Sasaram — with a handful of passionate students, a dream, and an unwavering belief in the transformative power of modeling and dance. What started as a small studio quickly blossomed into a movement.

Over the years, we organised multiple events, dance competitions, and modeling showcases across Bihar, building a formidable local presence and an unmatched reputation for grooming extraordinary talent.

Today, we arrive at the eternal ghats of Varanasi — a city of culture, energy, and aspiration — to open the doors of Elon Modeling Academy. Our mission: to help modeling aspirants shine here, and then, to carry their light to the national stage.

Our Mission

To discover, nurture, and launch the next generation of India's finest models.

Our Vision

To make Varanasi a celebrated hub of India's fashion and modeling industry.
