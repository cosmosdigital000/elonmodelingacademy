Our Story

From Sasaram to the Sacred Ghats

Our journey began modestly in Sasaram — with passionate students, a dream, and an unwavering belief in the transformative power of modeling and dance.

We organised multiple events, dance competitions, and modeling showcases across Bihar, building an unmatched reputation for grooming extraordinary talent.

Today, we arrive at the eternal ghats of Varanasi to open Elon Modeling Academy — helping aspirants shine locally and on the national stage.

Our Mission

Discover, nurture, and launch the next generation of India's finest models.

Our Vision

Make Varanasi a celebrated hub of India's fashion and modeling industry.
